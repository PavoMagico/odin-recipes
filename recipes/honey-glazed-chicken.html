Honey Glazed Chicken

This honey chicken recipe is a secured success on everyone's table. It's a light meal that everyone will enjoy, and they will probably ask you to cook it again! Serve with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sizes
As you can see, this dish doesn't require many ingredients, but it's important to adjust the amount you use of each one of them accurately so you can get the best results for you.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skiller over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
